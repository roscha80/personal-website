bio

Born in 1980 in Greifswald in East Germany, i finished school with middle maturity in 1996. After finishing training to become draftsman and worked in different companies. I started 2003 my career in the graphic world at Mediaschool in Waren, Muritz.
Working several years as a graphic artist in different fields and agencys around europe i became 2008 head of graphic at TVN GmbH, a TV and Movie production company in Hannover.
Since i worked over 7 years in the tv and movie industry i decided to get more into coding and finished successful the Academic Works Java Bootcamp in 2020.
As a graphic artist my love is to see and create beautyful things and i managed to get a place at the web development bootcamp at neuefische to learn more about frontend development.
Hopefully i will finish it successful in juli 2021.
